FUROBOT

A Pop Art Dialogue Between East and West

By Keng Fu Chu

The Exhibition

This exhibition showcases the signature works of Keng Fu Chu, a D.C.-based Taiwanese artist. Recognized as one of the top 150 illustrators worldwide by Illustration Now, Chu’s influential designs have been featured in premier publications across Taiwan, Korea, the U.S., Germany, and the U.K.

At the heart of this showcase is FUROBOT—a series of modern, playful robot sculptures that bridge the gap between imagination and contemporary design. Through these characters, Chu transforms cold mechanical forms into vibrant storytellers full of humor and life. We invite you to explore the world of FUROBOT and experience the creative vision of an artist who continues to shape the modern visual landscape.
